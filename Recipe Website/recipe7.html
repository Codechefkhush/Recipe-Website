Lentil Salad With Tahil Dressing

Pack four of your 5-a-day into each portion of this healthy lentil and sweet potato salad, which provides calcium, iron, folate, vitamin C and fibre.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : Veg Salad Healthy Nutritious

instructions

step 1

Heat the oil in a large non-stick wok. Add the sweet potato and fry for 5 mins, stirring frequently until it starts to soften. If it starts to brown too quickly, put a lid on the pan. Add the carrot, courgette, onion, cumin and ginger, then cook over a high heat, stirring frequently, until the veg is tender and a little charred. Stir in the seeds towards the end so they cook for a couple of mins.

step 2

Remove from the heat and add the lentils, bouillon powder, lemon zest, mint and parsley.

step 3

Meanwhile, stir the tahini with the garlic, yogurt and 1 tbsp water to make a dressing. Spoon the lentil salad into bowls and top with the dressing and paprika, if using.

ingredients

2 tbsp cold-pressed oil

320g sweet potatoes

2 large carrots

2 medium onions

1 tsp cumin seeds

1 tsp pumpkin seeds

1 garlic clove

tools

Large Baking Tray

Peeler

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
