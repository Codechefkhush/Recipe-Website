Paneer Butter Masala

This is one of the premium north Indian or Punjabi curries if not the king of the paneer curries. It is known for its mildly sweet and spicy taste with the goodness of fresh cream and butter topping with moist paneer. It is one of the most curries for most vegetarians and is prepared for lunch and dinner and served with freshly baked roti, chapati, naan and even a choice of rice. Paneer-based meals and curries are essential dishes to many vegetarians for their protein offerings.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Veg Punjabi Dinner Food

instructions

step 1

Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbsp butter, add 5 cloves, 2 pods cardamom.

step 2

Now add 2 onion, 2 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger and saute. Further add 3 tomato, 15 cashew and saute for 2 min. Cool it completely and transfer to the blender and blend to smooth paste.

step 3

In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil, 2 tbsp butter, 1 bay leaf and 1 chilli. Saute until the spices turn aromatic. Now add 1 onion and saute until onions turn and golden brown.

step 4

keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ¾ tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder, ½ garam masala and 1 tsp salt. Saute until the spices turn aromatic. Make sure not to burn the spices.

step 5

Add in prepared onion tomato paste and mix well. Cook until the oil separates from the masala paste. Now add 1 cup water and add in 2 tbsp cream and mix well. Also, add 16 cubes of paneer and mix gently.

ingredients

2 tbsp oil

2 pods cardamom

2 onion (sliced)

2 clove garlic

1 inch ginger

3 tomato (sliced)

15 cashew

1 tsp chilli powder

16 cubes paneer

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
